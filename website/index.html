PAGAMI

Create your BIP39 compatible mnemonic with a dice.

Check on GitHub!

How does it works?

Buy the hardware, flash the firmware and connect your board to a VGA monitor and PS/2 keyboard.


Turn on the board and follow instructions on the monitor.


Use a dice or a card deck to create the needed entropy.



Copy the mnemonic generate and store it in a safe place.


1. Buy hardware

You will need an ESP32 module with a VGA and PS/2 connectors. You can find on AliExpress for around 15$.

More Info

2. Flashing firmware

Open trentanove/trentanove.ino with Arduino IDE, configure for ESP32 board, select port and flash the firmware.

More Info

3. Use board

Connect a VGA monitor and PS/2 keyboard. Turn on the board and follow instructions on the monitor.
